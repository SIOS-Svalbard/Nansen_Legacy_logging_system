Nansen Legacy label printing


Flask application for priting labels that include a data matrix.

We use a data matrix to encode a universally unique identifier (UUID) for your sample.

UUIDs are valuable for providing unique identifiers for samples because they ensure each sample has a globally unique identification regardless of the system or location.

Even if a billion UUIDs were generated every second for the next 100 years, the probability of a single collision occurring would be less than one in a quintillion (10^18)!
